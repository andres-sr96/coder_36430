How our idea came to life


Hello, guys. Laila here.

Maybe you got some insights of how we started this web. Because the proffesor assign us as partnes in a project, we got to know each other.

We've got this videogames hobbie that we both could relate to. Stevie showed me his entery library of videogames and then I show him mine. Mine was better obviously. And that's where the idea come from. What if we create a webpage that we can login and put the games we've played and share our opinions with everybody. Give our personal rating; our thoughts about their soundtrack, gameplay, character development, etc.

So, My Game Library is our very first project we both, as partners, started and put a lot of effort on it. We wanted to share our opinions with everyone and that everyone could do it to. So please, feel free to share all of your thoughts.
